About Darwin

Darwin is an innovation platform that uses A.I. and crowdsourced expertise to make financing an innovation less risky and more transparent for both entrepreneurs and investors. In addition, Darwin’s A.I. has the ability to actively learn how entrepreneurs and investors make innovation and investment decisions making it easier to scale decision support.

The new way to showcase your Startup

The majority of startups fail before they even launch, and a significant percentage (over 90%) fail even after they've raised a round and launched a market solution.

Darwin has re-engineered how entrepreneurs go about launching their venture, validating it and raising innovation capital with the primary goal of enabling innovation to happen at a faster rate.
